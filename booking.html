About Me

As a Mexican American, I've learned to find beauty in every corner of life. I am self taught and have honed my skills over the course of tens of thousands of photos for the last 6 years.

Happy accidents is how I would describe my photographic style. The most important part of the shooting process is building a repertoire with the subject and learning what makes them unique. Once this is established, capturing this through my lens becomes trivial.

During our portrait session I will get you comfortable in front of the camera and direct your poses on your special day. I am only interested in capturing the most authentic you. Having worked with both experienced and first time models, you're in good hands.

My clients note that I am a master of adapting on the fly, having shot in everything from empty garages, churches, strip clubs, mosh pits, to 20,000 person arenas. Constantly looking to elevate, I thrive in uncertainty and chaos.

Let's capture your special day and make it last forever!
