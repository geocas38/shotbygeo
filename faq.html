Do you shoot video?

I am fully equipped to shoot video, having a gimbal, video lights, and drones on hand.

Although I prefer to shoot photos, I am always up for a challenge if the premise is interesting.

Can I bring a friend to the shoot?

Always.

My client's comfort is priority number one. Feel free to bring any friends to the shoot!

Can you send me the RAWs?

I do not give out RAW files outside of extenuating circumstances as I do not want to see my work butchered by others.

Quite frankly no one cares about my photos as much as I do, and I assure you I review every single individual pixel in my work during the editing process to ensure high quality output.

Can I use our shoot for commercial purposes?

This is a case by case basis. I love to support smaller artists the same way people put me on when I was just getting started, and for them there is no additional charge.

For the artists that can afford it, it is standard practice in the industry to offer a clause for buying commercial rights.

Message me on Instagram or email me with your intended use for the images as well as any further questions.

How long do you store the images for?

Depends on the size of the project as I have limited storage space, but I generally hold onto the images for 6 months.

How will you deliver the photos?

I will send a WeTransfer link on the promised delivery date for you to download at your convenience.

I use WeTransfer as it allows full, high resolution downloads. Instagram or Messenger compress the photos and lower the quality of the photos.

For mini shoots I can deliver the photos thru iMessage as well!
